Hi Friends! Welcome to Fred the Baker Baking Company. You can help me create donuts. To start, click on the oven image. As you increase the count of your donuts, you can spend them on hiring another baker or on job training which increases the efficiency of baking donuts. Be careful though, every 5 seconds, a donut becomes rotten and will be subtracted from your donut count automatically. Enjoy!

Fred the Baker Baking Company game was inspired by the Cookie Clicker game.
